2. Kansas Nebraska Act

Second on the list is the Kansas Nebraska Act. This act repealed the Missouri Compromise, allowed popular sovereignty, and formed two new territories. This act caused tons of proslavery and antislavery activists to storm into both of the territories and vote for what they wanted. The act is also responsible for the creation of the Republican Party, which was formed in response to the act. This created a much greater tension between the north and the south and greatly impacted their relationship, which lead more and more towards the Civil War.
