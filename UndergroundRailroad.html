5. The Underground Railroad

The Underground Railroad is number 5 on our list and was a system used often by Harriet Tubman, in her mission to save as many slaves as she could from slavery. The Underground Railroad had a big impact on the civil war because it in turn caused the south to want to form the fugitive slave act which allowed slave catchers to enter the north to retrieve slaves. This angered the North causing more separation between the North and South. Although this is number 5 on the list all of the events contributed a big amount leading towards the civil war.
