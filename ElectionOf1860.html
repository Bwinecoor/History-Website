1. Election of 1860

Number 1 on the List is the election of 1860. The election of 1860 was won by Abraham Lincoln when he beat his opponent John C Breckinridge. The election of 1860 was pivotal to causing the civil war because when Lincoln was elected, the South had essentially lost all of the power that they had. Before there were pro-slavery presidents that favored the south and kept slavery going there as long as possible. The South was afraid of Lincoln thinking he was a strong abolitionist, which made the south worried that he will push hard for slavery abolition.
